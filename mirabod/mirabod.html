Information about Apartment


New House's Mirabod Avenue apartment complex consists of modern and comfortable multi-storey (9-16) houses. The project of the residential complex envisages the construction of an underground car park, social facilities, as well as sports and children's playgrounds. The total area of two, three and four rooms in Mirabod Avenue is 37 sq.m. apartments from 119 sq.m. The complex is located in Mirabod district, on Fergana Yuli Street, near the Dostlik (former Uzbum) Park.
